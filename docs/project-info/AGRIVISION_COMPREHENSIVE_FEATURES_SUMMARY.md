# AgriVision: Comprehensive Agricultural Intelligence Platform for East Africa

## 🌍 Overview

AgriVision is a comprehensive agricultural intelligence platform specifically designed for East African farmers, featuring AI-powered insights, offline capabilities, multi-language support, and direct market access. The platform addresses the unique challenges of rain-fed agriculture, limited connectivity, and diverse linguistic requirements across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Ethiopia, Rwanda, Burundi, and DRC.
